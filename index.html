Welcome to our Infocomm Project - a chess GUI that runs the Stockfish engine. Our project aims to solve the lack of customisability when users play against chess bots online. We understand that playing against a chess bot with limited options can be frustrating and limiting. Therefore, we have created a chess GUI that allows users to customise almost every aspect of the Stockfish chess engine, from its strength to its playing style.
